🛰️ Wednesday Live — the Agentics Roundtable

A weekly drop-in video hangout for friendly netizens who are curious about AI agents. Think of it as the comment section come to life: we riff on the week's drip, swap what surprised us, argue (kindly) about where this is all headed, and help each other actually understand the hard parts. No experts-only vibe, no slides, no pitch — just good-faith humans talking.

🆓 Genuinely freeBuilt on Jitsi, the open-source video platform. No ticket, no signup, no download — click and you're in.
🗓️ Every Wednesday11:00 AM Pacific, about an hour. Drop in for five minutes or stay the whole time.
